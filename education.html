NSP National Means Cum Merit Scholarship 2024-25

Financial assistance for meritorious students to continue their secondary education

About The Program

The NSP National Means Cum Merit Scholarship Scheme 2024 is an initiative of the Department of School Education and Literacy, Government of India for students enrolled in Class 9 in government, government-aided, and local body schools. The scheme aims to provide financial assistance to meritorious students belonging to the EWS category to prevent dropouts and encourage them to continue their education at the secondary stage.

Scholarship Amount: ₹12,000 per annum

Eligibility

  • Must be an Indian national.
  • Enrolled in Class 9 in a government, government-aided, or local body school.
  • Scored at least 60% in Class 8 (5% relaxation for SC/ST students).
  • Annual family income must be less than ₹3,50,000.

Benefits

  • ₹12,000 per annum (₹1,000 per month).
  • A total of 1,00,000 scholarships will be awarded.
  • Amount is transferred directly to students' bank accounts via DBT.

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts & marksheets.
  • Income certificate issued by a competent authority.
  • Caste certificate (if applicable).
  • Aadhaar card & Bank details.
  • Disability certificate (if applicable).

Selection Criteria

Students must appear for a state-level test in Class 8 consisting of:

  • Mental Ability Test (MAT) - 90 minutes
  • Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT) - 90 minutes

Minimum qualifying score: 40% (Relaxed by 5% for SC/ST students).

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for the scholarship:

  1. Click the 'Apply Now' button below.
  2. Register/Login to the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
  3. Complete the One-Time Registration (OTR) process.
  4. Fill in the scholarship application form and upload required documents.
  5. Submit the application.
